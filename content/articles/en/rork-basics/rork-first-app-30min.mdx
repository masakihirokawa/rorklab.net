## What You'll Accomplish

Follow along with this article, and in 30 minutes you'll have a working smartphone app. Zero programming knowledge required.

In our previous article, "[No Code Needed! The Age of AI-Powered App Making](/en/articles/rork-basics/app-making-ai-era)," you learned what Rork can do. Now it's time to build something real.

## Step 1: Access Rork (5 minutes)

Open your browser (Google Chrome recommended) and go to:

```
https://rork.app
```

Click "Sign Up" or "Get Started" and register with your Google account or email.

```
Registration flow:
1. Go to rork.app
2. Click "Continue with Google"
3. Select your Google account
4. Accept the terms of service
5. Dashboard appears — you're ready!
```

## Step 2: Tell AI What to Build (5 minutes)

Click "Create New Project" on the dashboard. You'll see an AI chat interface.

Today we're building a **shopping list app**. Type something like this:

```
You: "Build a shopping list app with these features:
      - Add items by typing the name
      - Tap items to check them off
      - Button to clear all checked items
      - Clean, simple design
      - Soft blue color theme"
```

## Step 3: Watch AI Build Your App (5-10 minutes)

After you send your request, Rork's AI starts generating your app. You'll see the code and design being built automatically.

```
AI at work:
📱 Generating app screens...
⚙️ Building the add-item feature...
✅ Adding check-off functionality...
🗑️ Implementing delete feature...
🎨 Applying color theme...
✨ Complete!
```

In a few minutes, your app preview appears.

## Step 4: Try It on Your Phone (5 minutes)

Use the QR code to open your app on your smartphone.

```
How to test:
1. Find the QR code on the Rork screen
2. Scan it with your phone's camera
3. The app opens in your browser
4. Start using it!
```

Type an item name and tap "Add." Watch it appear in your list. Tap it to check it off. It works!

## Step 5: Refine and Improve (5 minutes)

Want changes? Just tell the AI.

```
You: "Make the text a bit larger.
      Also, add categories: Groceries, Household, Other."
```

```
You: "Let me enter quantity for each item too."
```

AI applies the changes and generates an updated version.

## Use Your Finished App

Your app's features:

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Is my app saved?

Yes, it's stored in your Rork account as a project. You can open, edit, and continue building it anytime.

### Q: Can I share my app with family?

Yes! Send the preview URL or QR code to family members, and they can use it on their smartphones.

### Q: What if my app doesn't work properly?

Tell the AI "_____ isn't working correctly. Please fix it." AI will identify the issue and apply a fix.

### Q: How many apps can I build on the free plan?

The free plan allows several projects. Start with one, and create more as you get comfortable.
