## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Rork AI truly no-code?

For basic apps, yes — you can build and publish without writing any code. For more complex features or customizations, having some programming knowledge helps you refine the generated code for higher quality results.

### Is Rork Companion available on the free plan?

Basic Companion features are available on the free plan. More advanced AI suggestions and real-time review capabilities are unlocked with paid plans. Check Rork's official site for current plan details.

### How accurately does Rork reproduce Figma designs?

Layout structure and component placement are reproduced with high fidelity. Complex animations or custom interactions may require manual adjustments. See the article's caveats section for specifics.
