## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I really build an app with zero programming knowledge?

Yes, really. You never write a single line of code. Describe what you want in plain language, and Rork's AI generates all the programming automatically.

### Q: Do I need a computer?

A computer browser is the primary way to use Rork, but you'll test your finished app on your smartphone.

### Q: Does it cost money?

Rork has a free tier. However, publishing to the App Store requires an Apple Developer account ($99/year). For personal use, there's no additional cost.
