**Category**: Rork AI ／ **Level**: Advanced

## 🥈 #2: A Complete Guide to Dynamic Island and Live Activities in Rork Max

**Category**: Rork Dev ／ **Level**: Intermediate

[A Complete Guide to Implementing Dynamic Island and Live Activities with Rork Max](/articles/rork-dev/rork-max-dynamic-island-live-activities-guide)

Dynamic Island and Live Activities (available on iPhone 14 Pro and later) let your app surface real-time information on the lock screen and in the Dynamic Island — without requiring the user to open the app. This guide walks through implementing delivery tracking, sports scores, and timer displays using Rork Max.

This kind of passive, always-available presence is one of the strongest drivers of user retention in modern iOS apps, and Rork Max makes it achievable without writing native Swift.
