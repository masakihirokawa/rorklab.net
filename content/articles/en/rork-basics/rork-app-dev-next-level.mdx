## Publish to the App Store

Sharing your app with the world means publishing it to the App Store.

### The Publishing Process

```
Steps to App Store publication:
1. Finish your app in Rork
2. Register for Apple Developer Program (~$99/year)
3. Use Rork's build feature to compile your app
4. Submit to App Store review
5. Once approved — anyone worldwide can download it!
```

Publishing to Google Play (Android) follows a similar process, also supported by Rork.

### Why Publish?

```
• People worldwide can use your app
• The pride of seeing YOUR app in the App Store
• Potential revenue from ads or in-app purchases
• Tell friends: "Search for it on the App Store!"
```

## Ways to Earn Money from Your Apps

### Method 1: Display Ads

Show advertisements within your app. This is the most common approach for free apps.

```
You: "Add an advertising system to this app."
```

### Method 2: Paid App

Set a price for your app — users pay to download it.

### Method 3: In-App Purchases / Subscriptions

Basic features are free; premium features require payment.

```
You: "Make the basic features free. Premium features
      (charts, data export) should cost $2.99/month."
```

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What if my app fails App Store review?

You'll receive specific feedback about why. Tell AI "My app was rejected for this reason. Please fix it." Most rejections require only minor adjustments.

### Q: Is app monetization realistic?

Many individual developers earn a few hundred to a few thousand dollars monthly from app ads. For bigger revenue, focus on building apps that solve real problems for users.

### Q: Should I learn programming now?

You don't need to, but if you're curious, start by reading the code Rork's AI writes. Ask "Explain this code line by line" and you'll naturally absorb the fundamentals.
