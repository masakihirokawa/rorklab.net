## What Is Rork? — The AI-Powered App Development Platform

Rork is an AI platform that automatically generates native apps from natural language descriptions. Founded in early 2024, its team includes former Google engineers and Y Combinator alumni.

**What Rork does:**
- Generate iOS and Android apps without writing a single line of code
- Complete the entire workflow—from design to App Store publication—in hours
- Handle API integrations, database design, and deployment automatically

Traditional app development involves designers, frontend engineers, and backend engineers working for months. Rork can deliver the same result in days. This efficiency has made it the darling of startups and indie developers worldwide.
