Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced developer, Rork accelerates your development timeline significantly.

## Rork Max Launch — The Next Generation Platform

The most tangible sign of this growth is the launch of Rork Max, representing the evolution of the platform.

### Key Improvements in Rork Max

**Native App Support**
Rork Max expands beyond React Native to support SwiftUI (iOS) and Jetpack Compose (Android). This means you can build apps with the level of polish and performance that users expect from native apps.

**Direct App Store Publishing**
Previously, submitting apps to the App Store and Google Play involved complex workflows. Rork Max streamlines this entirely—you can now publish directly from the platform, eliminating a huge source of friction for indie developers.

**Multi-Model AI Support**
You're no longer locked into a single AI provider. Rork Max supports Claude, Gemini, and GPT-4o, letting you choose the best model for your project's specific needs.

**Integrated Ecosystem**
Design collaboration (Figma integration), backend services (Supabase, Firebase), analytics, and third-party APIs are all built in. Everything you need is accessible from a unified interface.

## Startup Features That Actually Matter

Here's where Rork becomes genuinely powerful for indie entrepreneurs.

### App Store Submission Support

Rork Max handles the entire submission workflow: Developer Program registration, certificate management, privacy policy setup, and pre-submission compliance checks. What previously required bouncing between multiple tools and pages is now a streamlined process.

### Revenue Features

Once your app is live, Rork provides native support for monetization:

- **In-App Purchases**: Paid upgrades and feature unlocks
- **Ad Integration**: Built-in AdMob support
- **Subscriptions**: Monthly and annual subscription logic
- **Payment Processing**: Stripe integration for direct revenue capture

These aren't afterthoughts—they're first-class features designed into the platform.

### Rork Companion

Recently released, Rork Companion is a native Mac app that bridges cloud development with local build workflows. It dramatically speeds up testing and compilation, letting you iterate faster without waiting for cloud builds.
