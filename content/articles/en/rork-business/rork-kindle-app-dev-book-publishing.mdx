Amazon Kindle Direct Publishing offers you a direct path to fill this gap. Authors publishing Rork guides are seeing:

- **User acquisition funnel**: Book readers become customers of your apps
- **Compound revenue**: A single well-written guide becomes evergreen passive income

This guide walks through the complete workflow: from research to published book, then leveraging readers into multiple revenue streams.

## Creating Publication-Ready Content

### File Format Requirements

```
【Kindle Submission Files】

Format options:
- EPUB (recommended): Universal device support
- MOBI: Older Kindle models
- PDF: Fixed layout (lower price tier)

Best choice: EPUB format

Technical specifications:
- Encoding: UTF-8
- Images: 300 DPI minimum (body), 150 DPI (illustrations)
- Total file size: 500MB maximum
- Cover image: 1600×2400 pixels (3:2 aspect ratio)
```

### Cover Design

2. **Professional Designer** (¥3,000-10,000)

   - Custom, high-quality results
   - Worth the investment for premium positioning

3. **AI Image Generation + Text** (¥0)
   - Midjourney or DALL-E for primary image
   - Add title/subtitle in Canva
   - Modern, eye-catching aesthetic

## The KDP Publishing Process

### Step 1: KDP Account Setup

```
1. Visit Amazon KDP (kdp.amazon.com)
2. Sign in with Amazon account (or create one)
3. Click "Create a new title"
4. Complete identity verification (required for Japan)
```

### Step 2: Book Details & Metadata

```
【Critical Fields】

Title: "Build iOS Apps in 30 Days with Rork Max"
Subtitle: "From Zero to $10K Monthly App Store Revenue"
Author: [Your Name]
Description: (200-300 words explaining value)
  - Emphasize the gap this book fills
  - Highlight practical, actionable content
  - Mention target audience benefits

Categories (select 3):
- Technology > Programming
- Business > Entrepreneurship
- Computers > App Development
- Education > Professional Skills
```

### Step 3: Pricing Strategy

**Option A: KDP Unlimited Program**
- Price range: ¥980-4,980
- Exclusive to Amazon (cannot sell elsewhere)
- Earn royalties on page reads (KENP)
- Perfect for long-form educational content
- Recommended for first-time authors

**Option B: Standard Publishing**
- Price: ¥2,480 (fixed)
- Can sell on other platforms
- Higher per-unit royalty (35% for ¥2,480)
- Less passive income from reads

**Recommendation**: Start with KDP Unlimited. Monthly page read earnings provide steady income regardless of sales fluctuations.

### Step 4: Manuscript Upload

```
1. Cover image: Upload 1600×2400 JPG/PNG
2. Manuscript: Upload EPUB or MOBI file
3. Preview: Check formatting on Kindle device preview
4. Verify: No text overflow, images aligned, TOC working
```

### Step 5: Publication

```
1. Review all metadata one final time
2. Click "Publish Your Book"
3. Confirmation email received
4. Within 12-24 hours: Book appears in Kindle Store
5. Monitor: First reviews and reader feedback
```
