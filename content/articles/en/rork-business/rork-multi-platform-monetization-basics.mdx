## Introduction — Why Multi-Platform Monetization Matters

Building an app with Rork is exciting. But once you've created it, the question becomes clear: "How do I actually make money from this?"

In the past, app developers relied primarily on App Store and Google Play sales or in-app purchases. However, with Rork in 2026, everything has changed. You can now generate revenue from a single app concept across multiple platforms simultaneously.

In Part 1, we'll explore **5 major revenue channels** available for Rork apps, along with their characteristics, how to get started with each, and realistic revenue potential. Part 2 will dive into concrete implementation strategies using AI design tools like Canva and Figma.

## Chapter 1: Five Revenue Channels for Rork Apps

### Channel 1: App Store and Google Play

The most direct path to income is app store distribution. With Rork, an app you built in hours can reach billions of users worldwide.

**Ad-Supported Model**
- Implement Google AdMob or Unity Ads in free apps
- 1,000 daily active users (DAU) generates ¥30,000–¥100,000/month
- 5,000 DAU reaches ¥150,000–¥500,000/month
- With Rork's productivity, building a portfolio of multiple apps creates steady ad revenue

**In-App Purchases**
- Subscription pricing: ¥300–¥980/month
- 100–500 subscribers = ¥30,000–¥490,000/month
- Rork Max includes StoreKit 2 integration for simple implementation

**Premium (Pay-Once) Model**
- Price niche apps at ¥250–¥1,500
- Examples: specialized calculators, industry-specific checklists
- Multiple apps can reach ¥50,000–¥300,000/month

### Channel 2: Web App Version with Stripe Checkout

Release your Rork app as a web version accessible via browsers. Use Stripe to process payments and unlock an additional revenue stream.

**Advantages:**
- Avoid Apple's 30% App Store fee
- Flexible subscription management
- Organic SEO traffic brings additional users

**Code Example:**
```javascript
// Stripe integration in Next.js
import Stripe from 'stripe';

export default async function POST(req) {
  const stripe = new Stripe(process.env.STRIPE_SECRET_KEY);
  const session = await stripe.checkout.sessions.create({
    line_items: [{
      price: process.env.STRIPE_PRICE_ID,
      quantity: 1,
    }],
    mode: 'subscription',
    success_url: 'https://example.com/success',
    cancel_url: 'https://example.com/cancel',
  });
  return Response.json({ id: session.id });
}
```

Build web versions quickly using Next.js and Cloudflare Workers.

### Channel 3: YouTube — App Demos and Development Logs

Showcase your Rork app on YouTube to earn **both app downloads and YouTube ad revenue**.

**Content Ideas:**
- "I built this app in X hours" production videos
- App tutorials and walkthroughs
- "Rork Max + [Tool]" series content

**Revenue Estimates:**
- 100,000 video views = ¥10,000–¥30,000/month in ad revenue
- Each video links viewers to your app
- As your channel grows, download rates from videos increase

### Channel 4: Kindle — Publish Your Rork Knowledge

Transform your Rork app development expertise into an ebook and publish on Kindle Direct Publishing. Higher price points and better royalty rates than template sales.

**Example Titles:**
- "Rork Max to App Store: Beginner's Step-by-Step Guide" (¥980)
- "Build an AI Chatbot with Rork + Gemini API in 5 Steps" (¥1,500)
- Sales of 50–100 copies/month = ¥50,000–¥150,000/month

Amazon takes a 35% cut, but KDP Select offers read-through bonuses for additional income.

### Channel 5: Digital Products — UI Templates and Project Files

Sell your Rork app design and project files on platforms like Gumroad, BOOTH, or your own site.

**Template Examples:**
- Habit tracker template (¥3,000)
- Recipe management starter kit (¥5,000)
- HealthKit-integrated fitness app (¥7,000)
- Stripe-integrated ecommerce app (¥10,000)

**20–50 sales/month = ¥60,000–¥500,000/month** in passive income. Buyers can instantly customize templates with prompts, making them valuable as ready-to-deploy solutions.

## Chapter 5: Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: Does managing multiple revenue channels require much extra work?

**A:** Initial setup takes effort, but it reduces over time:
- **App Store/Google Play**: 1–2 updates/month
- **Web + Stripe**: Auto-deploy via Vercel, 1–2 updates/month
- **YouTube**: 1–2 videos/month
- **Templates**: Fire-and-forget (digital products have no inventory)

Distributing across channels often requires less effort than focusing on a single channel. Rork's auto-testing and deployment features further reduce workload.

### Q2: Will App Store and Google Play reviews take longer if I submit simultaneously?

**A:** No—reviews happen in parallel:
- **App Store**: 1–3 days (average 24 hours)
- **Google Play**: Near-instant (usually a few hours)

Rork Max's 2-click publishing can automate simultaneous submissions.

### Q3: Are there copyright issues with selling templates?

**A:** Code generated by Rork belongs to you. However:
- Check Rork's terms of service (typically permit this)
- Include license notices if using third-party libraries
- Clearly define end-user rights in your terms

With these safeguards, legal risk is minimal.
