## Introduction — App Side Businesses Transform with AI Combinations

"I want to earn from apps, but coding is hard" — Rork Max has dramatically lowered this barrier. You can build native Swift apps just by giving AI instructions.

But covering the entire journey from development to release to monetization with a single AI is challenging. Successful indie developers use Gemini for planning, Rork Max for building, and Claude for quality assurance — three or more AIs working in concert.

## AI Role Distribution

### Rork Max — The App Builder

Rork Max generates native Swift code and builds App Store-ready apps through AI. UI design, logic implementation, API integration — all through prompts. The 2-click App Store publishing flow means you don't need Xcode expertise.

### Gemini — Market Research and ASO

App success depends on idea quality. Use Gemini's Google Search integration to research app category demand, competitor ratings and downloads, and search keywords. Gemini also excels at ASO keyword selection.

### Claude — Quality Assurance and Content

Review Rork Max-generated code with Claude Code for early bug detection and optimization. Create app descriptions, privacy policies, and terms of service with Claude.

### Antigravity — Backend Development

When your app needs server-side features (authentication, database, push notifications), build APIs with Antigravity.

## Revenue Model Selection

### Freemium (Free + Paid Features)

Offer basic features free, monetize advanced features. Low download barrier maximizes user acquisition. With Rork Max's StoreKit 2 support, In-App Purchases are AI-assisted. 200 subscribers at $5/month generates meaningful revenue.

### Paid Apps

Utility apps specializing in specific functions sell steadily at $2–5. Camera filters, template editors, specialized calculators — target niche demand.

### Ad Revenue

Integrate Google AdMob into free apps for download-proportional income. 10,000 monthly DAU typically generates $200-400/month.

## Practical Workflow — Idea to Release

### Step 1: Ideation and Market Research (Gemini)

Ask Gemini about high-demand iOS app categories in 2026, cross-reference with Google Trends data. Analyze App Store reviews to find pain points in existing apps.

### Step 2: Prototype Development (Rork Max)

Build an MVP with Rork Max. Focus on minimum viable features and get a working app quickly. Ship fast, gather feedback, iterate.

### Step 3: Quality Improvement (Claude + Antigravity)

Review prototype code with Claude Code for performance and security issues. Build backend APIs with Antigravity if needed.

### Step 4: ASO and Launch (Gemini + Claude)

Keyword research with Gemini, app descriptions and metadata with Claude. Publish to both App Store and Google Play for maximum reach.

### Step 5: Ongoing Operations (All AIs)

Post-launch: review analysis (Gemini), feature additions (Rork Max), quality improvements (Claude). Apps that respond quickly to user feedback earn better ratings and more downloads.

## Success Patterns

Meditation timer app (freemium) — Simple UI via Rork Max with premium ambient sounds and guided sessions. 300 monthly downloads, 5% conversion.

QR code scanner (ad revenue) — Focused utility app generating steady ad income with just one week of development time.

Pet health tracker (subscription) — Rork Max + Supabase backend with AI health advice as the premium feature.
