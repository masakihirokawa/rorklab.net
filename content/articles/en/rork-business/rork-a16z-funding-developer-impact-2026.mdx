## Why a16z Invested in Rork

As covered in [What Is Rork](/articles/rork-basics/what-is-rork), Rork is an AI coding and no-code tool for building iOS and Android apps using natural language. So why did a16z commit $2.8M at pre-seed?

Three reasons stand out.

First, **extraordinary product-market fit**. $1.5M ARR in 3 days is rare even by Y Combinator standards. Products that "sell themselves from day one" are solving genuine pain points.

Second, **bottom-up growth**. Most of Rork's users are indie developers and small teams who spread the word through social media. a16z has a strong track record of backing this kind of viral, community-driven growth.

Third, **massive market opportunity**. The mobile app market exceeds $400B annually. As AI democratizes app creation, the platform that wins developer adoption stands to become a dominant force.
