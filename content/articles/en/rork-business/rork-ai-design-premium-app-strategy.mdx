## Introduction — Design Drives Revenue

In [Part 1](/articles/rork-business/rork-multi-platform-monetization-basics), we explored expanding revenue channels beyond the app store. Now in Part 2, we'll use AI design tools to elevate app quality and implement **premium monetization** strategies.

Two apps with identical features will perform vastly differently based on design quality. The better-designed one wins on download rates, retention, and conversion to paid plans. With tools like Figma and Canva, solo developers can achieve professional-grade UI.

Generate app promotional websites with Stitch. Build landing pages with download links, feature highlights, and screenshot galleries in minutes.

## Designing Your Premium Pricing Model

### The Freemium Approach

Offer core features free, charge for advanced functionality.

```javascript
// Premium pricing tier design
const pricingTiers = {
  free: {
    name: "Free",
    features: [
      "All basic features",
      "Ads enabled",
      "Data storage: 10 items",
    ],
    price: "$0",
  },
  pro: {
    name: "Pro",
    features: [
      "All basic features",
      "Ad-free experience",
      "Unlimited data storage",
      "Dark mode",
      "Custom themes",
      "Priority support",
    ],
    price: "$3.99/month or $29.99/year",
  },
  lifetime: {
    name: "Lifetime",
    features: ["All Pro features", "Permanent access", "All future updates included"],
    price: "$79.99 (one-time)",
  },
};

console.log(JSON.stringify(pricingTiers, null, 2));
```

### Subscription vs. One-Time Purchase

| Factor | Subscription | One-Time |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| Initial Revenue | Lower | Higher |
| Long-Term Revenue | Higher (recurring) | Limited |
| User Psychology | "Let me try it" | "Do I really need this?" |
| Dev Motivation | High (updates drive revenue) | Can decline |
| Best For | Tools, habit apps | Complete apps |

For indie developers, a hybrid model — subscription plus lifetime purchase — works best. Monthly subscriptions provide steady income while the lifetime option creates occasional revenue spikes.

## Why Design Quality Increases Conversion Rates

The data is clear on design's impact on revenue.

**First Impressions**: Users judge app quality in just 0.05 seconds. Poor design leads to immediate uninstalls.

**Retention**: Apps with polished UI show roughly 2x higher 30-day retention rates compared to poorly designed alternatives.

**Willingness to Pay**: Refined design creates a perception of value, directly increasing premium conversion rates.

## ASO (App Store Optimization) for Discovery

Even the best premium app needs to be found.

**Title and Subtitle**: Include keywords naturally while clearly communicating your app's value proposition.
**Screenshots**: Beautiful Canva-designed screenshots significantly boost download conversion rates.
**Description**: Go beyond feature lists — clearly state the benefits users will experience.
**Review Management**: Thoughtfully responding to user reviews improves your store rating over time.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Figma free to use?

Yes. Figma's free plan is generous enough for individual use — you can create up to three Figma files and three FigJam files. Upgrade to a paid plan when you need team collaboration features.

### What features should I put behind the paywall?

The sweet spot is features that are "nice to have but not essential." Ad removal, customization options, unlimited storage, and advanced analytics are proven premium features.

### What's a good free-to-paid conversion rate?

Industry standard is 2–5%. Above 10% is exceptional. To improve conversion, ensure free users experience enough value that upgrading feels like a natural next step.

### What if I'm not confident in my design skills?

Browse Figma Community for free UI kits created by professional designers. Customize these components as your foundation — you'll achieve high-quality UI even with limited design experience. Canva templates work similarly well for marketing materials.
