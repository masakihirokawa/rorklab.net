## What Is Canva × Claude?

In January 2026, Canva launched its AI connector for Claude, enabling users to create, search, edit, and export Canva designs directly from Claude's chat interface.

This integration is powered by **MCP (Model Context Protocol)**, allowing Claude to securely access your Canva account and manage design operations on your behalf.

<Callout type="info">
**What the Canva MCP Connector Can Do:**
- Generate new designs from text prompts
- Search and browse existing designs
- Edit text and images
- Apply brand kits for on-brand designs
- Autofill templates with data
- Export to PDF, PNG, PPTX, and more
- Manage and organize folders
</Callout>

## Setup

### Connecting on Claude.ai

1. Log in to Claude.ai
2. Click the connector icon in the chat input area
3. Select **Canva**
4. Log in with your Canva account and grant access

Once connected, you can access Canva features directly from Claude's chat.

### Connecting on Cowork

The Canva connector is also available in Cowork. Add it from the settings panel and link your Canva account.

## Basic Usage

### Creating New Designs

Simply describe what you want in natural language.

```
"Create a 10-page presentation for a tech company
using blue and white colors. The topic is AI use cases."
```

Claude invokes Canva's design generation AI to create the presentation. You'll see design candidates to choose from, and your selection is saved to your Canva account.

### Searching Existing Designs

```
"Find the marketing materials I created last week"
```

Claude searches your Canva account and displays matching designs.

### Editing Designs

```
"Change the title on page 3 of this presentation
to 'Q1 Performance Report'"
```

Text replacement, image swaps, and formatting changes — all from chat instructions.

### Exporting

```
"Export this design as a PDF"
```

Supports PDF, PNG, JPG, PPTX, GIF, and MP4 formats.

## Brand Kit Integration

One of the most powerful aspects of this integration is access to Canva's brand kits directly from Claude.

### What Are Brand Kits?

Canva brand kits store your brand colors, fonts, logos, and other brand assets in one place.

### Generating On-Brand Designs

```
"Using our brand kit, create an Instagram post
announcing our new product release"
```

Claude displays available brand kits, lets you select one, then generates designs that follow your brand guidelines.

<Callout type="tip">
**Brand consistency:** Specifying a brand kit ensures colors, fonts, and logo placement automatically conform to guidelines. Even when team members create designs separately, output remains consistent.
</Callout>

## Template Usage

### Searching Brand Templates

```
"Find a monthly report template"
```

Searches and displays brand templates saved in your Canva account.

### Template Autofill

Automatically populate templates with data.

```
"Fill this template with this month's sales data:
Revenue: $120K, MoM growth: +15%, Target achievement: 108%"
```

Works with templates containing charts and graphs too.

## Practical Workflows

### Workflow 1: Weekly Report Automation

1. Ask Claude to "create a report summarizing last week's project progress"
2. Claude selects a brand template
3. Generates the design based on information provided in chat
4. Review and adjust content
5. Export as PDF for sharing

### Workflow 2: Batch SNS Content Creation

1. Ask "create 5 social media posts for next week about our new feature release"
2. Claude generates designs for Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook
3. Adjust text and images for each
4. Export as PNG

### Workflow 3: Presentation Creation and Refinement

1. Ask "create a presentation for next week's board meeting"
2. Claude proposes an outline, generates design after approval
3. Fine-tune: "change the chart on page 5 from bar to pie chart"
4. Export as PPTX for final adjustments in PowerPoint

- **Social Media**: Instagram posts, Twitter/X posts, Facebook covers, YouTube thumbnails
- **Documents**: Reports, proposals, memos
- **Marketing**: Flyers, posters, business cards, invitations
- **Others**: Logos, infographics, wallpapers, photo collages

## Tips and Best Practices

### Always Review

Always review AI-generated designs before publishing. Check for text errors and subtle deviations from brand guidelines.

### Be Specific

Specific instructions produce better results than vague ones.

**Before:** "Make a nice poster"

**After:** "Create a portrait A3 poster with a blue gradient background and white text reading 'AI Summit 2026'. Date: March 15, Venue: Tokyo Big Sight. Include a QR code placeholder at the bottom."

### Leverage Canva Pro

Some advanced features (premium templates, multiple brand kit management) require Canva Pro.

## Summary

The Canva × Claude integration enables anyone to create professional designs through chat alone, regardless of design tool expertise. The brand kit integration is particularly powerful for maintaining consistent design quality across teams.

If you regularly create presentations or social media images, give this integration a try.

## FAQ

### Does it work with Canva's free plan?

Yes, basic features work with Canva's free plan. However, premium templates and advanced brand kit features require Canva Pro.

### Which Claude plan supports this?

The connector feature is available on Claude.ai Pro plan and above. It's also available in Cowork.

### Who owns the copyright for generated designs?

This follows Canva's terms of service. Designs created in Canva are available for commercial use (subject to Canva's asset licensing terms).
