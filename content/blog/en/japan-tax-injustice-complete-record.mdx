## The Trap — How AdMob Tax Classification Destroyed My Finances

### Your AdMob Revenue Is Export-Exempt — Did Anyone Tell You?

Google AdMob revenue is paid by **Google Ireland Ltd** — an Irish entity. Under Japan's Consumption Tax Act, this is an international transaction classified as **export-exempt (non-taxable)**.

Apple App Store revenue is paid by **Apple Distribution International Ltd** — also Irish. Also **non-taxable**.

**Neither my tax accountant nor the tax office ever told me this.** I paid approximately **¥5 million ($33,000) in consumption tax I never owed** over several years.
