## The Tech We're Proud Of

Dolice Labs isn't just a blog. It's built on an ambitious technical foundation:

- **Next.js 16** with App Router for modern, performant architecture
- **TypeScript** for type safety and maintainability
- **Cloudflare Workers** for global edge delivery
- **next-intl v4** supporting fully bilingual content (English + Japanese)
- **MDX-to-JSON** transformation for dynamic content handling
- **Stripe integration** for sustainable membership pricing

We could have used a simpler stack. Instead, we chose complexity in service of quality and scalability. That bet is paying off.

100,000 requests flowing through a well-designed system feels as good as the metric itself.
