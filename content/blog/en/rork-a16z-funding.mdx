## Rork Secures $2.8M Funding from a16z

We're excited to announce that Rork has secured **$2.8 million in Series A funding** from Andreessen Horowitz (a16z), one of the world's most influential venture capital firms. This investment validates our mission to democratize mobile app development and accelerates our path to transforming how developers build applications.

### The Rork Team Behind the Vision

Rork was founded by **Levan Kvirkvelia** and **Daniel Dhawan**, two visionary entrepreneurs who recognized a fundamental problem: building mobile apps is too hard and too slow. With their combined expertise in mobile development, infrastructure, and product design, they set out to create a platform that would make app development accessible to everyone.

### Remarkable Growth Metrics

Since inception, Rork has experienced explosive growth:
- **743,000+ monthly visitors** demonstrate the massive demand for our platform
- **85% growth rate** shows how rapidly the developer community is adopting Rork
- **Cross-platform reach** spanning iOS and Android through our React Native approach
- **Multiple tiers** serving everyone from hobbyists to enterprise teams

### What This Funding Means

The $2.8M investment from a16z provides Rork with the resources to:

**Expand Our Team:** We're hiring world-class engineers, designers, and product managers who share our vision of democratizing app development.

**Accelerate Product Development:** With additional resources, we can ship features faster, improve our AI capabilities, and expand platform support across more ecosystems.

**Scale Infrastructure:** As our user base grows to hundreds of thousands, we need robust infrastructure to serve our global community reliably.

**Market Expansion:** This funding enables us to reach developers worldwide and establish Rork as the go-to platform for rapid app development.

### The Rork Advantage

Our platform combines several key advantages:

**Speed:** Generate and deploy apps in hours, not weeks. Our AI-powered code generation eliminates boilerplate and accelerates development.

**Accessibility:** You don't need to be a React Native expert to build professional apps. Rork handles the technical complexity.

**Cost-Effective:** Starting at just $25/month, Rork makes professional app development affordable for startups, freelancers, and enterprises alike.

**Quality:** Apps built with Rork are production-ready, performant, and maintainable. They're not just prototypes—they're real apps for real users.

### Our Mission: Democratizing Mobile Development

For decades, building mobile apps has been the domain of specialist developers with years of experience. We believe that should change. Great ideas deserve to become great apps, regardless of the technical background of the person building them.

Rork empowers:
- **Entrepreneurs** to bring their app ideas to market faster
- **Designers** to build interactive prototypes without learning to code
- **Developers** to focus on unique business logic rather than boilerplate
- **Enterprises** to accelerate their mobile development pipeline

### What's Next

With a16z's backing and our growing community of creators, we're more focused than ever on our vision. Our roadmap includes:

- Enhanced AI capabilities for more complex app logic
- Expanded platform support (web, desktop, and more)
- Better integration with popular backend services
- Advanced testing and deployment automation
- Community tools and marketplace for shared components
