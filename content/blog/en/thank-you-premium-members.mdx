## Important: Restoring Access on a New Browser or After Clearing Cookies

Here's something important for all our members to know.

If you **switch to a different browser** or **clear your cookies**, you may temporarily lose access to premium content. But it's easy to get back in:

1. **Visit the [Support page](/support)** — Click the link above to reach our support page
2. **Enter your purchase email** — Type the email you used when buying your membership via Stripe
3. **Submit** — Your premium access will be restored immediately

Takes just seconds. If you have any questions or run into issues, please don't hesitate to reach out.
