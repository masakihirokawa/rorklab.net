### What is Rork Max?

Rork Max is powered by Claude Code and Claude Opus 4.6, enabling developers and non-developers alike to generate fully functional native Swift applications without touching Xcode. The platform supports all Apple platforms—iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, Apple TV, Vision Pro, and iMessage—giving you unprecedented reach across the entire Apple ecosystem.

### How It Works

The workflow is browser-based and intuitive. Simply describe what you want your app to do. Claude understands your requirements and generates production-ready Swift code that compiles in our cloud Mac fleet. No local Xcode installation, no complex build processes, no steep learning curve. Your app is ready in minutes, not weeks.
